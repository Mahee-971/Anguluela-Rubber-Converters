Who We Are

Anguluela Rubber Convert Pvt Ltd

Anguluela Rubber Convert Pvt Ltd is a Sri Lanka-based natural rubber processing company specialising in technically specified rubber grades for industrial and tyre manufacturing applications.

We source raw latex from trusted smallholder farmers across Sri Lanka's rubber belt, process it through state-of-the-art facilities, and deliver consistent, quality-certified rubber bales to clients who demand the highest standards.

With a maximum monthly capacity of 100 metric tons and a growing international presence, we are now actively expanding our supply partnerships into the European market — with our European representation based in Hungary.
